About JUIT

JUIT was conceived by a joint vision of the Govt of Himanchal Pradesh and theSSS Founder Chairman of Jaypee Group Shri Jaiprakash Gaur in 2000. Land was provided on lease by the State Government and the university was established by Act No of 2002 via Extraordinary Gazettw Notification of the Government of Himanchal Pradesh dated May 23, 2002. The University Grants Commission accorded its approval under Section 2(F) of the UGC Act vide their Letter No. F 9-10/2002(CPP-1) dated 09 Dec 2002. The JUIT is also a member of the association of Indian Universities(AIU).
